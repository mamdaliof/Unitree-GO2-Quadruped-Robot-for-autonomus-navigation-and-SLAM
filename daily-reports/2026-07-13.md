# Daily Report - 2026-07-14

## 🔬 Literature & Paper Reviews Completed Today
### Mobile Structural Health Monitoring Based on Legged Robots
* **Paper Notes:** [[literature/Robot Type/Mobile Structural Health Monitoring Based on Legged Robots]]
* **Key Findings:**
  * **Inspection Advantage:** Quadruped robots (specifically the IDOG platform based on Unitree) exhibit superior maneuverability, traversability, and equilibrium stability compared to wheeled UGVs or fewer-legged robots, making them highly suited for structural health monitoring (SHM).
  * **Vibration-Based SHM:** Vibration data is gathered using mobile accelerometers which do not require strong adhesives (unlike strain gauges) or thermal insulation (unlike temperature sensors).
  * **Measurement Grid Normalization:** Stitches multiple local measurements into a continuous structural mode shape using an overlapping **"leapfrog" strategy**. For a grid of $m$ locations, robots conduct $m-1$ setups where each new setup shares one identical physical location from the previous setup. This shared baseline allows normalization of changing scales caused by fluctuating ambient forces (wind, traffic).

## 🗂️ Workspace Audit: CMU Autonomy Stack for Go2
* **Audit Report:** [[docs/literature_reviews/autonomy_stack_go2_audit]]
* **Architecture Overview:** [[docs/literature_reviews/cmu_autonomy_stack_overview]]
* **Real-Robot Tutorial:** [[docs/literature_reviews/real_robot_tutorial]]
* **Key Insights Extracted:**
  * **SDK Control:** Utilizes high-level `unitree_api::msg::Request` commands on `/api/sport/request` DDS topic rather than low-level motor joint PD control.
  * **Sensor Calibration & Filters:** Point-LIO SLAM uses a physical pitch mount tilt of `15.1°` (upside-down `164.9°` Euler rotation) and discards points inside a chassis bounding box to avoid self-reflection noise.
  * **Simulation Setup:** Detailed the ROS-TCP-Endpoint bridge and Unity environment execution (documented in [[docs/literature_reviews/simulation_guide]]).
  * **Bugs & Limitations:** Audited known drawbacks including high L1 LiDAR noise below $0.3\text{ m}$, SLAM drift requiring manual terrain map resets, 10-20 seconds DDS startup latency, and unsynchronized camera stream timestamps.
